我的CS61A笔记（一）

发表于2026-02-04|更新于2026-02-04
|浏览量:|评论数:

谁还记得那位北大学长知名的《CS自学指南》？我好像把它和大学前的假期的野心勃勃一起抛掷脑后很长一段时间了。我突然想到他在大一的迷茫是被CS61A驱散的——灵光一现——我去简单调查了MIT，Harvard, UC(Berkley)的CS本科课程。很多启发类的课程，这使我确实对我与世界顶尖的教育差距出现了微弱的感触。我想：我为什么不能爱CS呢？

我打算敲的第一扇门是Python，用CS61A。

我还没有忘记博客共享的精神，于是我会有意识地把笔记写得有助于任何人更好地利用CS61A这一资源，更简便地得到最舒适的知识。尽管我的博客目前保持私密。

我也需要利用博客笔记本的功能，所以，我可能再某些地方采取双语，这是我的兴趣。

In recalling CS61A, I experienced a moment of clarity—sudden and illuminating. It reminded me, almost magically, of a time when I felt genuinely inspired by what lay ahead, driven by curiosity to understand how things worked, and consistently focused on moving forward. That inner voice resonated clearly: “You still have passion.” It felt like a necessary reminder, a guard against trivial distractions.

I also feel compelled to share what I have learned. With that in mind, an important goal of mine is to make CS61A more accessible and intellectually original for Chinese learners.

At the same time, I see an opportunity for personal growth. Whether through crafting bilingual explanations or treating the writing itself as deliberate practice in English, I aim to make the process meaningful also for myself.
