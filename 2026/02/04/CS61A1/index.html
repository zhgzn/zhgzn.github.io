Lecture 1

It’s quite a basic beginnning. Whoever has alredy got exopsed to codes should have known about Expressions.

An Expression describes a computation and evaluates to a value.

All expressions can use function call notation.

Evaluation procedure for call expressions:

1.Evaluate the operatorandthen the operand subexpressions

2.Apply the function that is the value of the operator subexpression to the arguments that are the values of the oprand subexpressions

That’s what Lecture 1 all about.

Lab 0: Getting Started

The official website of CS61A offers perfect introductions. It may be hehindhand and cumbersome, yet with keeping up with official website, you are sure to handle that.

I spent a bunch of time to find out what CS61A is made of, so I see it as a neccessety to clearify it.

1.Lectures or Videos, what is very accessible on YouTube and Bilibili, are main content of the class.

2.Live Lectures are for their groupwork, Q&A and so forth that are THE class’, UCB students’ things. They also conduct teaching meybe same things as videos, but it’s said that “This(Video) is the most common way for people to see the lecture material in 61A”. And it’s not available unless you have an account for UCB.

3.Textbook is the videos in text format. That is to say, to choose either videos or textbook is enough for CS61A learning.

4.Webiste is the most useful resource for you get all projects and homework here.

Lab 0 per se comes as the very first attempt, which is easy. Disc 0 is also easy if you are ever exposed to codes.
